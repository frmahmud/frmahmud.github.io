Hi, This is Firoz Mahmud.

I have been working as a Data Analyst at the Department of Social Services since November 2024. My responsibilities include:

  • <anaging the complete data and analytics lifecycle, covering extraction, analysis, and modelling, to drive data-informed decision-making and meet departmental reporting obligations.
  • Establishing and validating critical data parameters, including setting variables, testing values, and defining/verifying value labels to ensure consistent data integrity and usability across all projects.
  • Leading data and intelligence projects, coordinating with internal stakeholders to define requirements, ensure data governance, and deliver tailored project outcomes.
  • Communicating complex insights by producing accessible materials, including dashboards, visualisations, and reports, tailored for various audiences and decision-makers.

Prior to joining the Department of Social Services, I served as an Analyst at Seeing Machines. Previously, I was a Graduate Researcher in Computer Science at the University of New South Wales (UNSW) at the Australian Defence Force Academy (ADFA) in Canberra, Australia. Before moving to Australia, I worked in Bangladesh as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). Alongside my teaching duties, I also served as a CCNA Instructor at the Cisco Local Academy, RUET, and as an ICT Trainer at the Bangladesh Computer Council (BCC).
